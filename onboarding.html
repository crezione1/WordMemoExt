Welcome to WordMemo! 🚀

WordMemo is a powerful Chrome extension that helps you learn English vocabulary effortlessly while browsing the web. Let's take a quick tour to show you how it works and set up your preferences.

Select & Save

Simply highlight any word on any webpage to save it to your personal dictionary

Auto Translation

Get instant translations in your preferred language with context

Smart Learning

Words appear highlighted on future visits to help reinforce your memory
